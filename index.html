Monkey D. Luffy

Captain of the Straw Hat Pirates and leader with a boundless dream to become the Pirate King.

Luffy ate the Gomu Gomu no Mi, granting him rubber-like abilities.

His unbreakable spirit inspires his crew throughout countless adventures.

  • Nickname: Straw Hat Luffy
  • Devil Fruit: Gum-Gum Fruit Gomu Gomu no Mi
  • Wanted Bounty: 3,000,000,000 Berries

Roronoa Zoro

The swordsman of the Straw Hat Pirates renowned for his powerful three-sword style.

Zoro aspires to become the world's greatest swordsman.

  • Santoryu (Three Sword Style) practitioner
  • Favorite attack: Oni Giri
  • Infamous for getting lost
// Zoro's Three Sword Style
santoryu.attack("Oni Giri");

Zoro often naps, trains, and enjoys a challenge.

Nami

Nami is the navigator of the crew, famous for her weather prediction and map-making skills.

Her ultimate goal is to draw a complete map of the world.

  • Title: "Cat Burglar"
  • Special weapon: Climatact
  • Known for: Extreme love of money
// Nami summons a thundercloud
nami.useClimatact("Thunderbolt Tempo");

Nami joined after Luffy rescued her from Arlong Park.

Her navigational skills have saved the Straw Hats many times.

Usopp

Sharp-shooting sniper and skilled inventor of the crew.

Usopp's lies often become reality as the story unfolds.

  • Alias: "Sogeking" (Sniper King)
  • Signature weapon: Kabuto
  • Dream: Become a brave warrior of the sea
// Usopp fires a special shot
usopp.shoot("Firebird Star");

He provides comic relief and inventive solutions.

Usopp is proud of his role as Straw Hat's sharpshooter.

Sanji

Sanji is the cook of the Straw Hat crew, and a master of martial arts based on strong kicks.

He dreams of finding the legendary All Blue sea.

  • Known as: "Black Leg Sanji"
  • Expertise: Cooking and Kick-based martial arts
  • Famous move: Diable Jambe
// Sanji prepares a feast
sanji.cook("All Blue Seafood Risotto");

Sanji's devotion to great food and his friends is unwavering.

He often feuds with Zoro but cares deeply about the crew.
